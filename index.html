AI Just Made Businesses a Whole Lot Smarter

New research reveals how Artificial Intelligence is transforming decision-making across industries.

By Staff Writer • October 17, 2025

AI systems are helping businesses become more competitive.

A new study by researchers at Zarqa University reveals that businesses using Artificial Intelligence (AI) with Business Intelligence (BI; tools that analyze data for businesses) to streamline their operations are seeing more overall competitiveness and efficiency.

According to the research, published in the TEM Journal, companies that incorporated AI into their systems saw a 52% increase in performance. This is supported by a strong correlation between AI and more accurate decision making.

Smarter, Faster Decisions

Business Intelligence (BI) software helps companies analyze data, while Artificial Intelligence (AI) predicts patterns and outcomes. When combined, they transform raw data into real-time insights managers can use.

The researchers explain that this integration allows companies to predict trends, automate analysis, and reduce decision-making time. The combination of AI and BI “leads to an improvement in the overall effectiveness of the organization.”

Real-World Applications

  • Customer Service: AI models analyze feedback and recommend responses and actions.
  • Education: Adaptive learning systems track quiz results and attendance to tailor lessons.
  • Finance & Marketing: Tools manage risk, personalize campaigns, and forecast supply/demand.

These uses show how AI and BI together make organizations more efficient and resilient in rapidly changing markets.

Challenges Ahead

Despite the benefits, this integration requires high-quality data and strong cybersecurity protections. Additionally, many employees need training to use the technology effectively. Organizations that adapt quickly are better equipped to gain long-term advantages.

Why It Matters

The researchers conclude that combining AI and BI is more about staying competitive than simply improving software.

“Integrating AI and business intelligence goes a long way in enhancing organizational effectiveness. These technologies help organizations make timely and more optimal decisions to cope with the fast pace of the market.”
