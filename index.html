AI Just Made Businesses a Whole Lot Smarter

New research reveals how Artificial Intelligence is transforming decision-making across industries.

AI systems are helping businesses become more competitive.

From factories to top firms, AI is changing the way businesses operate, think, and compete.

A new study by researchers at Zarqa University reveals that businesses combining Artificial Intelligence (AI) with Business Intelligence to streamline operations are seeing more competitiveness and efficiency.

According to the research published in the TEM Journal, companies that incorporated AI into their systems saw a 52 percent increase in performance.

This is supported by a strong correlation between AI and more accurate decision making.

Smarter, Faster Decisions

The research paper states that utilizing AI leads to an improvement in overall organizational effectiveness.

AI allows businesses to predict trends, automate analysis, and reduce decision-making time.

"Businesses are now able to make smarter, data-driven choices in real time," said lead researcher Mohammad Al-Momani.

Real-World Applications

  • Customer Service: AI models analyze customer feedback and recommend responses to businesses.
  • Education: Adaptive systems track quiz results and attendance to tailor lessons for students.
  • Finance and Marketing: AI tools manage risk, personalize campaigns, and forecast demand changes.

These uses show how AI and BI together make organizations more efficient and resilient in rapidly changing markets.

Challenges Ahead

Despite the benefits, this integration requires high-quality data and strong cybersecurity protections.

Additionally, many employees need additional training to use the technology effectively.

Organizations that adapt quickly are better equipped to gain long-term advantages.

Why It Matters

The researchers conclude that combining AI and BI is about staying competitive rather than improving software.

"Integrating AI and business intelligence goes a long way in enhancing organizational effectiveness. These technologies help organizations make timely and more optimal decisions to cope with the fast pace of the market."

Final Thoughts

Organizations that use both human insight and AI are redefining what it means to make smart decisions.

For businesses, the question is how fast can they implement AI to stay competitive.

At a Glance

  • Study Title: Integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Business Intelligence (BI): A Framework for Improving Enterprise Performance
  • Published: TEM Journal, August 2025
  • Authors: Mohammad Musa Al-Momani et al.
  • Institution: Zarqa University, Jordan
  • Key Finding: Companies that use both AI and BI improve performance and decision-making speed.
